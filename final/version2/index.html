Plant Parenthood

Can you keep the plant alive?

Congrats! It's a plant!

Welcome to the wonderful world of parenthood! Your best friend has entrusted you to take care of their emotional-support plant while they are out of town. You must watch over it for 14 days, keeping it alive and healthy.

Each day, the plant might lose a bit of its health so you must choose to either feed the plant or let the plant rest. Be careful! Too little or too much of a resource will hurt the plant.

Don't worry, you're only responsible for their favorite thing in the whole entire world. Good luck and no pressure!
