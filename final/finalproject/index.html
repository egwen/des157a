Plant Parenthood

Can you keep the plant alive?

Welcome to the wonderful world of parenthood!

Your best friend is going out of town and they have entrusted you to take care of their emotional-support plant. The plant is in perfect health, and your goal is to keep their plant alive and as healthy as possible when they return.

Don't worry, you're only responsible for their favorite thing in the whole entire world. Good luck and no pressure!

Plant Care 101

At the start of the game, the plant is perfectly healthy with just the right amount of water , sun , and nutrients .

Each day, the plant might lose a bit of each resource and you must decide whether to FEED the plant random resources or let the plant REST by giving it nothing.

Be careful! Too little or too much of a resource will hurt the plant.
